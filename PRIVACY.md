# SVExtensions Privacy Policy

SVExtensions develops Magento and Adobe Commerce extensions designed to enhance store functionality.

We respect the privacy of merchants and their customers.

## Data Collection

SVExtensions extensions do not collect, transmit, or store personal data outside of the Magento or Adobe Commerce installation where the extension is installed.

All processing occurs locally within the store environment.

## Data Usage

Extensions may access Magento internal data such as:

- Store configuration settings
- Customer segments
- Catalog configuration
- Storefront settings

This data is used strictly for extension functionality.

## Third-Party Services

SVExtensions extensions do not send any data to external servers unless explicitly stated in the extension documentation.
